Gift Response: The House of Blue Walls

Course — Exploratory Making

Kevin gave me the gift of sharing his mother's old Canon PowerShot compact camera that he found while cleaning out and downsizing his family home. He said he treasured the photos of his grandmother the most, because she's no longer with us. His gift inspired me to think about only a week ago, when I was in Virginia doing the final touches to put my grandmother's house on the market, as she passed away almost a year ago and none of us have the capacity to keep the home.

I'm a person who always takes photos and videos. But I'm also a person who loves to look back at those photos and videos and remember all of the moments. So Kevin gave me the gift of looking back and remembering.

I used timelapse on my iPhone, then CapCut to edit and add photos.

— KJB
